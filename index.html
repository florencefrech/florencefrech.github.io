Summary

Florence graduated from the University of Notre Dame in 2008 with a Finance Major. After graduation, Florence moved to Chicago and joined HSBC's 2-Year Financial Management Rotational Program where she learned by rotating through four different divisions of the bank. In 2010, Florence returned to El Salvador and joined The Network Company, a boutique regional investment bank, where she focused primarily on Mergers and Acquisitions. She currently lives in Evanston, IL where she is pursuing her MBA at the Kellogg School of Management.
